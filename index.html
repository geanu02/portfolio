My Work

Who I am

Web Developer based in NYC

I have chosen to be a web developer because I am passionate about bringing my creative ideas to life. My journey began at the age of 15 when I started building static websites on Geocities and experimenting with CSS on Multiply. I was captivated by the beauty of websites and often envisioned how I could recreate them in my unique style.

After eight years working as a computer systems analyst in Silicon Valley and dedicating a year to learning data science, AWS architecture, and mobile development, I made the decision to finally follow my passion for frontend web development. Recently, I completed an intensive 10-week Full-Stack Engineering bootcamp with Coding Temple, which significantly enhanced my frontend skills, including React, TypeScript, Tailwind, and modern web stacks like Flask + Python, SQLAlchemy + Postgres, and RESTful APIs. I am enthusiastic about taking on more projects and embarking on my career as a frontend developer!
